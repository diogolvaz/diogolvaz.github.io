Diogo Lopes Vaz

diogo.vaz@tecnico.ulisboa.pt

Diogo Vaz is a Ph.D. student and Teaching Assistant at Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), Lisboa, Portugal, and a Ph.D. student researcher in the Distributed Systems Group (GSD), at INESC-ID. His main interests are in Distributed Systems, Machine Learning and Cybersecurity, more precisely, he's researching about using machine learning techniques to solve distributed problems. He was also involved on the DE4A european project, as a software developer from INESC-ID.

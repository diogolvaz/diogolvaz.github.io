Diogo Lopes Vaz

Diogo Vaz is a PhD student and Teaching Assistant at Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), Lisboa, Portugal, and a junior researcher in the Distributed Systems Group (GSD), at INESC-ID. His main interests are in Distributed Systems, Machine Learning and Cybersecurity, more precisely, he's researching about using machine learning techniques to solve distributed problems. He is also involved on the DE4A project, as a software developer from INESC-ID.
